Healthy Habits for your Health

Everyone wants to lead a healthy life. Here is some tips to get a healthy life-
1. Drink 3-4 litre water per day,
2. Eat fruits and veggies,
3. Avoid street Foods,
4. Getting regular exercise,
5. Get your sleep timely,
6. Avoid smoking,
7. Manage Stress.

If you able to follow those tips, then you will get a much happier healthy. One more thing that help you to keep yourself healthy and happy is laughter. Laughter is usually a sign of happiness. Some advantages of laughter is - when laughing , the brainn also releases endorphies that can relieve some physical pain. Laughter also boosts the number of antibody-producing cells, leading to a stronger immune system.
